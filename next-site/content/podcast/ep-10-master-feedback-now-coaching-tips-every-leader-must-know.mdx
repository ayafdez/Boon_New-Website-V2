## Show Notes:

### **Guest: Cathy Manginelli**

In this episode, Cathy Manginelli, Chief Talent Officer at TriNet, discusses performance reviews, coaching, and feedback.

Discover how leaders can harness the power of coaching to unlock team potential, drive productivity, and create a feedback-rich culture. Whether you're a seasoned executive or a new manager, these tips will help you lead with confidence and transform your team's performance.

 Here are some key takeaways from this episode:

- **Good feedback can boost performance**. Learn how effective feedback can transform your team’s productivity.

- **The difference between coaching and consulting.** Understand the key differences and when to apply each for better leadership.

- **Self-awareness is your leadership superpower**. See why knowing your strengths and blind spots is crucial for growth.

 
Ready to elevate your leadership game? Tune in and start transforming your team today!
