## Show Notes: Turn Generational Differences Into Assets

### Guest: Shee Shee Van Inwegen

Cross-generational communication skills are quickly becoming a coveted superpower in the workplace.

And with more generations now working together than ever before, challenges in this same area are at an all-time high.

These challenges are understandable, no matter which generation you identify with. But with a little empathy and some effort, they can be overcome.

Or, better, turned into assets.

In this episode, veteran leadership coach Shee Shee Van Inwegen walks me through how to bridge divides between employees of all ages.

I think this is a really important topic. Enjoy the chat and let us know if you have any questions or anything to add!

- Alex

### **In this episode, we learn:**

- How to cultivate psychological safety at work

- Why and how workplaces should embrace diversity

- How to tailor your generational communications approach to individual preferences

- How to personalize care at scale to foster collaboration across generations.
