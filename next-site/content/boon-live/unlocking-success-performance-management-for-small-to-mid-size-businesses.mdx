As businesses navigate the complexities of the ‘new normal,’ effective performance management has become a cornerstone for success. This engaging webinar, led by TriNet Chief Talent Officer Cathy Manginelli, Bennie’s People Operations Manager Kia Castilow, and Boon CEO Alex Simmons, explores how small and mid-size businesses can maximize both employee potential and business outcomes.

### Why Performance Management Matters More Than Ever

This isn’t just for large corporations. It’s a vital tool for businesses of every size, fostering alignment, personal growth, and organizational success. The panelists emphasized a shift from traditional metrics to a more holistic approach—one that integrates personal well-being with professional development.

Key trends include continuous feedback, moving beyond annual reviews to ongoing conversational performance discussions, a whole-person focus, recognizing the interplay between personal and professional lives in today’s hybrid workplaces, and manager empowerment by providing tools and training to equip managers for empathetic and effective leadership.

### Key Takeaways from the Webinar

Firstly, modernizing performance management involves transitioning from outdated practices to personalized, forward-looking strategies that emphasize development over review. Embedding company values into performance management fosters a culture of accountability and engagement. Flexible solutions for small businesses, such as integrating feedback into everyday workflows, can make a significant difference.

### Practical Steps to Boost Performance Management

The webinar offered actionable advice, particularly for small and mid-size businesses. Start small and keep it simple. Focus on clear goal-setting, consistent feedback, and coaching to build momentum. Equip managers with tools and training to enhance their ability to lead with empathy and confidence. Introduce a blend of formal evaluations and informal conversations to create a balanced and practical approach.
